Research Excellence & Innovation

Join LASIGE Summer of Research: Shaping Tomorrow's Researchers

Hands-On Research Experience

Our 3-month summer program (July-September) offers undergraduate and first-year master students direct involvement in cutting-edge research projects, with mentorship from LASIGE researchers.

Comprehensive Training

Gain valuable skills through workshops covering research fundamentals (literature reviews, academic writing) and technical training (machine learning, data analysis).

Supportive Community

Become part of a vibrant research environment with academic and social events, building connections with fellow students and experienced researchers while discovering your research potential.
