Research Excellence & Innovation

Join LASIGE Summer of Research: Shaping Tomorrow's Researchers

LASIGE is a premier research center at the Faculdade de Ciências da Universidade de Lisboa, recognized with the highest excellence rating from Portugal's Foundation for Science and Technology. Here, we're not just conducting research — we're shaping the digital future through groundbreaking work.

Within our community of 250+ researchers at the University of Lisbon, you'll find yourself contributing to seven Research Lines of Excellence that address real-world challenges, from cyber-physical systems and healthcare informatics to secure decentralized systems and inclusive human-computer interaction. LASIGE advantage extends beyond our labs. Our researchers regularly publish in top-tier journals, secure competitive European funding, and collaborate with industry partners that transform theoretical advances into tangible solutions. Many of our students have presented at international leading conferences, building professional networks that have led to positions at international organizations, such as Google, Intel, Meta, and Microsoft, and top universities worldwide.

Don't just study the future of computing — help create it at LASIGE, where we're not just pursuing excellence in computer science and engineering — we're redefining it.

Hands-On Research Experience

Our 3-month summer program (July-September) offers undergraduate and first-year master students direct involvement in cutting-edge research projects, with mentorship from LASIGE researchers.

Comprehensive Training

Gain valuable skills through workshops covering research fundamentals (literature reviews, academic writing) and technical training (machine learning, data analysis).

Supportive Community

Become part of a vibrant research environment with academic and social events, building connections with fellow students and experienced researchers while discovering your research potential.
